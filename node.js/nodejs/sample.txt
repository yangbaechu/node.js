The act of dropping out of a science high school, foreign language high school, international high school, autonomous private high school, dropping out of an autonomous high school or transferring to a general high school, and entering the TO in the middle. In this case, the transfer of a school is usually similar to the transfer of a university. What's different from university transfer is that the transfer test is the same as the entrance exam for new students. Unlike universities that can be transferred only in the third grade, they can be transferred to the second and third grades, and there is no need to drop out because they are transferred from a former high school.
